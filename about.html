The TransH@ckFeminist (THF!) camp, organised in collaboration between Calafou & /etc (eclectic tech carnival) will take place from Monday 4th - Monday 11th August 2014 at Calafou, just outside the village of Vallbona d'Anoia 60km to the west of Barcelona, Spain.

Call for proposals

THF! Anarcha Server & Gynepunk technologies anti-patriarcal autonomous server & Do it with others (DIWO) gynecological biolab
TransHACKfeminist! camp (THF!) Calafou, in collaboration with / etc (eclectic tech carnival), is pleased to open this call for proposals to all transfeminist collectives, queer, trans, fags, bollos (?), bitches, hackers, genderhackers and unicorns.
